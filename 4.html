PUNCH CARD SYSTEM

Joseph Marie Jaccquard,a French textile weaver,used the principle of the weaving process to represent the two digit of the binary system.Jacquared took a large step in the development of computers when he developed punch card to increase rug production.In 1801,Jacquard invented a power loom with an automatic card reader known as punch card machine.The idea of jacquard to use punched cards to provide an effective means of communication with machines.He automamated the process with the use of punched cards and placed them between the needles and thread.The presence or absence of hole represented the two digit of binary system,which is the base for all modern digital computers.
