COLOSSUS

In 1994,British matematician Alan Mathison,along with some colleagues ,created a computer named colossus, which comprised 1800 Vaccum tubes.It was one of the world's earlist working programmable electronic digital computers. Colossus was a special - purpose machine that suited a narrow range of tasks (for example, it is not capable of performig Decimal multilications). Although colossus was built as a special - purpose computer,it proved flexible enough to be programmed to execute a variety of different routines. .
